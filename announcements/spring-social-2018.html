  • 6:30 - 7:30 - Socialize

  • 7:30 - 8:30 - Laura Adams presentation
  • 8:30 - 9:00 - Questions

Well known professional ACMG guide and international explorer Laura Adams has spent her adult life exploring remote landscapes around the globe. In the past year she has completed four expeditions to the Arctic exploring the west coast of Greenland, sailing through the North-West Passage, supporting a team of scientists researching the Arctic Archipelago, and skiing among the big walls of Baffin Island. Join Laura as she shares her experiences and stunning images of this remote and rapidly changing polar ecosystem: its landscape, peoples and unique climate.
