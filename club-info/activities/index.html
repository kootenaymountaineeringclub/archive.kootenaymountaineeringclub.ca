KMC Activities

The Outings Schedule Page

Along with the summer hiking camps and the winter ski camp, the Kootenay Mountaineering Club provides a summer and winter calendar of mountain outings. Hikes, bike rides, cross-country skis, backcountry skis, mountaineering, snowshoe outings and even the occasional kayak outing.

Mountain School

The KMC has a history stretching back to its inception of providing instructional opportunities for our members to increase the depth of their knowledge and experience in the mountains.

Summit Register Program

The Kootenay Mountaineering Club has a summit register program that provides funds for registers to be placed on Canadian mountains.

Trip Reports

It's always a good idea to share your experiences and adventures in the mountains with other members.
