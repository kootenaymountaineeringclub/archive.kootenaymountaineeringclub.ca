Members receive the link for the 2014 Summer Trip Schedule, including coordinator contact information via email. Please contact the Summer Trip Director if you don't have it.

Notes for Non-Members: You can find the schedule here: Summer Trip schedule. (Google Docs)

Most club trips are open to both members and non-members, but trip coordinators may choose to limit their trips to club members. Non-members are welcome to try one or two club trips, before joining the club. If you are interested in joining one of our trips as a guest, please contact the Summer Trip Director who will put you in contact with the trip coordinator. Contact the coordinator at least three days prior to the trip and read the guidelines for trip participants. View the trip grading scale to see if the trip you want to join fits within your personal capabilities. Print out, read and sign our standard liability waiver. Give it to the trip coordinator on the day of the trip. You will not be allowed to join a KMC trip without a signed waiver.

Enjoy your trip with the Kootenay Mountaineering Club!
