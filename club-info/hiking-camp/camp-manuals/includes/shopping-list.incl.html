Bread

Whoever picks up the bread Thursday or Friday before camp calls the store beforehand to confirm the order.

Ordering bread

  • Inform baker that bread MUST be completely cool before bagging to prevent sweating.
  • Request bread be packed in sturdy boxes with lids. Banana boxes work well. Not flats as they are too bulky.
  • If bakery can't do it, box it yourself.
